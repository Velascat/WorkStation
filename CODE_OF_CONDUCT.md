# Code of Conduct

This project follows the [Contributor Covenant v2.1](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/).

## Summary

Contributors are expected to act professionally, treat others with respect, and create a welcoming environment for everyone regardless of background, experience level, or identity.

## Enforcement

Instances of unacceptable behavior may be reported to the project maintainers at **coding.projects.1642@proton.me**. All reports will be handled confidentially.

Maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing standards of acceptable behavior and may take corrective action — including removing, editing, or rejecting contributions — in response to behavior that violates this code.
